Assumptions & Methodology ▼
  • Data Source: Jira REST API v3 (search/jql) at redhat.atlassian.net.
  • Restricted Projects: RHOAIENG, RHAIENG, and INFERENG require Jira authentication and are not included in automated data collection.
  • Aging WIP Definition: Issues currently in the “In Progress” status category whose last update timestamp is more than 14 days ago.
  • Flow Ratio (Little’s Law): WIP count × 30 / monthly throughput. Provides an approximation of average cycle time in days.
  • Release Risk Thresholds:
    • Green: >70% complete or >30 days remaining until release.
    • Yellow: <70% complete and <30 days remaining, or no release date set and <70% complete.
    • Red: <50% complete and <14 days remaining, overdue releases that are not 100% complete.
  • Target Version is read from custom field customfield_10855 (cf[10855]).
