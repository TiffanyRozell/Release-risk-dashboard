Assumptions, Data Notes & Risk Logic

Data Source: Jira Cloud (redhat.atlassian.net) — projects AIPCC, INFERENG, RHAIENG, RHOAIENG.

Version Tracking Field: Target Version (customfield_10855, JQL: "Target Version"). This is used instead of fixVersion because fixVersion is typically not assigned until after work is completed. Some projects have limited Target Version adoption; fixVersion was used as fallback where Target Version returned no data (noted in version rows).

Release Risk Thresholds:

  • Critical: <50% done AND <14 days remaining, OR past due with open work
  • At Risk: <70% done AND <30 days remaining
  • On Track: >70% done OR >30 days remaining
  • Complete: 100% done (or only 1-2 items remaining)

Aging WIP: Issues with statusCategory = "In Progress" whose last update was more than 14 days ago. These represent potential stalls or blocked work.

WIP-to-Throughput Ratio: Current in-progress items / items resolved in last 30 days. Ratio >1.0 means WIP exceeds monthly throughput (risk). Ratio <0.5 is healthy.

Created vs Resolved: Net positive means backlog is growing; teams are taking on more than they complete.

Issue Types: Epics and Features are excluded from all counts (only Story, Task, Bug, Spike, Sub-task, etc.).
