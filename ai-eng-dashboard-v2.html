Assumptions & Methodology ▼
  • Data Source: Jira REST API v3 (search/jql) at redhat.atlassian.net, queried 2026-03-25.
  • Sprint velocity is calculated as issues completed / total issues in sprint (percentage-based) from the last 6 closed sprints.
  • Risk thresholds (Sprint): Green = current sprint scope within +10% of average historical total. Yellow = 10–30% over average. Red = >30% over average or completion rate <50% of historical average.
  • Risk thresholds (Release): Green = >70% done or >30d remaining. Yellow = <70% done and <30d remaining. Red = <50% done and <14d remaining, or overdue.
  • Target Version is read from custom field customfield_10855 (cf[10855]).
  • Project Mapping: RHAIENG maps to RHELAI; RHOAIENG maps to RHODS. INFERENG was not found in the Jira instance.
