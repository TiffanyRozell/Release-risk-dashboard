Assumptions & Methodology ▶
  • Data source: Jira REST API v3 (unauthenticated) at redhat.atlassian.net, queried 2026-05-27.
  • Projects queried: AIPCC (AI Platform Core Components), RHELAI (Red Hat Enterprise Linux AI), RHODS (Red Hat OpenShift Data Science). The requested keys INFERENG, RHAIENG, and RHOAIENG do not exist in this Jira instance; RHELAI and RHODS are the closest matches.
  • Version issues: Queried using "Target Version" (customfield_10855), not fixVersion. Only versions with ≥1 issue are shown.
  • Sprint data: Board and sprint APIs require authentication. Kanban-style 30-day throughput is used instead.
  • Risk calculation (Release):
    • Green: ≥70% done OR >30 days remaining
    • Yellow: <70% done AND <30 days remaining, or progress is borderline
    • Red: <50% done AND <14 days remaining, or overdue
  • Risk calculation (Sprint/Team):
    • Green: WIP:Throughput ratio <1.0 and aging WIP <15%
    • Yellow: Ratio 1.0–3.0 or aging 15–50%
    • Red: Ratio >3.0 or aging >50%
  • Aging WIP: Issues in "In Progress" statusCategory with last update >14 days ago.
  • Throughput: Issues resolved in the last 30 days.
  • WIP count note: AIPCC WIP (614) differs from aging WIP (51), indicating active work. RHELAI and RHODS show near-100% aging, suggesting these projects may have moved to other trackers or are in maintenance mode.
